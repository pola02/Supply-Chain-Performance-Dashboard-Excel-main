# 🚚 Supply Chain Performance Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

An interactive **Supply Chain Performance Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze key supply chain operations, monitor KPIs, identify operational bottlenecks, and support business decision-making through data-driven insights.

The dashboard transforms raw supply chain data into meaningful visualizations, enabling stakeholders to evaluate warehouse performance, transportation distribution, operational costs, order fulfillment, and monthly demand trends.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Supply chain performance directly impacts customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and business profitability.

This project focuses on analyzing supply chain operations by tracking:

- Total Orders
- Total Quantity
- Total Cost
- Average Delay Days
- Warehouse Performance
- Transportation Analysis
- Country-wise Cost Distribution
- Monthly Order Trends
- Monthly Quantity Trends
- Order Status Analysis
- Executive Business Insights

The dashboard was designed with an executive reporting approach, allowing decision-makers to quickly identify trends and operational improvement opportunities.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance
- Track monthly order and quantity trends
- Analyze warehouse workload distribution
- Evaluate transportation methods
- Compare operational costs across countries

- Support strategic business decisions
